Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Magnus Firearms ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at magnusfirearms.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a quote request — name, email address, phone number (optional), caliber, and thread pitch.
  • Subscribe to our newsletter — email address and phone number (optional).

We do not collect payment information through this website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your quote requests and inquiries.
  • Send you newsletters and promotional updates you have opted into.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • EmailJS — used to transmit form submissions to us via email. Your submission data passes through EmailJS's servers. See EmailJS's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — used to collect anonymized usage data (pages visited, session duration, device type). Google Analytics does not receive your name, email, or phone number. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was provided or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time by replying "unsubscribe" to any email we send.

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to distinguish users and sessions. You can disable cookies in your browser settings or use a browser extension to opt out of Google Analytics tracking.
